Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known celestial body to harbor life. It has oceans, landmasses, and a vibrant biosphere.

Mars

Known as the Red Planet, Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun. It's a target for future space missions and holds clues to past water presence.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System. Famous for its swirling clouds and massive storms, it's a gas giant with many moons.
